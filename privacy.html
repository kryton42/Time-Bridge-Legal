Introduction

Time Bridge is designed with privacy in mind. This page explains what information is handled when you use the app.

What We Collect

Time Bridge may store app preferences on your device (for example, display settings and recent timezone choices) to improve your experience.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data and do not share it with data brokers.

Data Retention

Locally stored app settings remain on your device until you change settings, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

TimeBridge is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
