TimeBridge Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

Introduction

TimeBridge ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use TimeBridge.

Important: TimeBridge is designed to minimize data collection. We do not sell personal data and we do not use your data for advertising.

Data Collection and Usage

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account information (no sign-in required)
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location tracking for advertising
  • No personal data sold to third parties

What May Be Stored Locally on Your Device

  • App preferences (for example: selected time format, theme, recent settings)
  • Recently used conversion inputs or timezone selections (if enabled in app behavior)

This data is stored on your device to provide app functionality and improve your experience.

Permissions and Access

TimeBridge only requests permissions needed for app features. If a permission is requested, it is used only for that feature and not for tracking.

You can manage permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

TimeBridge does not sell personal data and does not share your data with data brokers.

If future versions include optional third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting), this policy will be updated before those changes are released.

Data Retention

Data stored on your device remains until you clear app data, remove settings, or uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

TimeBridge is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
