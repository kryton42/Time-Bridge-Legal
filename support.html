Frequently Asked Questions

What is TimeBridge?

TimeBridge is an app that helps you convert time zones and schedule events across different regions quickly and clearly.

Does TimeBridge collect my personal data?

TimeBridge is designed with privacy in mind. If you contact support, we only use your email to reply to your request.

I found a bug. What should I include in my email?

Please include your iPhone model, iOS version, app version, and steps to reproduce the issue.

Can I request new features?

Yes. Feature requests are welcome by email and help guide future updates.

Privacy & Security

TimeBridge is built to minimize data collection and keep your usage private. For full details, please read the Privacy Policy.
